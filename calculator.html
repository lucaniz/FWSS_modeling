attoFIL/gas
Formula (from Lotus source): effective_gas_price = BaseFee + min(GasPremium, GasFeeCap − BaseFee)
Total tx cost = GasUsed × BaseFee (burned) + GasUsed × effective_premium (to miner) + overestimation burn on unused gas above 10% tolerance.
Today (pre FIP-0115) with BaseFee ≈ 100 attoFIL, the miner premium dominates (~99.9% of the fee is burned as burn+tip, but mostly tip). Under FIP-0115 (Draft), BaseFee would rise to ~90% of EGP — burn dominates, miners earn less tip. Use the toggle below to model both scenarios.
